C) Delivery And COD Services
  1. Perfect Line Sports Third Party Courier Will Perform The Following Services To The Sole Benefit Of Customer:
    1. Collect Customer’s Shipments From Various Locations As Agreed Upon Between The Parties In Writing;
    2. Provide Customs Clearance Services For Applicable Goods;
    3. Deliver The Shipments To Consignee’s Address As Provided By Customer/Vendor;
    4. Collect The COD Amount, As Provided In The Invoice By Customer/Vendor, From The Consignee Upon Delivery Of Shipment To The Consignee (In The UAE Only); And
    5. Remit The Collected COD Amount To Customer.
  2. The Total Product Charge For Such Goods, Exclusive Of Taxes, Shipping, Handling, And Customs Duty, Will Not Exceed USD 500.
  3. The COD Amount Will Be Collected By The Perfect Line Sport Products LLC Through One Of Its Affiliates Or Third Party In An Amount Equal To The Amount Stated In The Invoice Provided By Customer/Vendor. The Parties Agree That The COD Amount Stated In Customer/Vendor Invoice Will Be In Local Currency And The COD Amount Collected Will Be In Local Currency. In The Event The Consignee Refuses To Pay COD Amount, Then Perfect Line Sport Products Will Not Be Liable For COD Amount Not Collected From Consignee Due To Consignee’s Refusal To Pay COD Amount.
  4. Perfect Line Sport Products LLC , Directly Or Through One Of Its Affiliates Or Third Party, Will Remit The Consolidated Amount Of The Collected COD Amount On Weekly Basis Or By Agreed With Vendors.

  2. Pre-Dispatch Procedure: Perfect Line Sports Will Directly, Or Through Its Third Party/Affiliate Will Provide Customer/Vendor With Clicktoship (Desktop Application Used To Manage Bulk Shipments), Which Can Be Used For:
    • Creating Shipping Labels
    • Shipments Manifest
    • Reports
    • Tracking Shipments
  3. Clicktoship: Clicktoship Enables Customer/Vendors To Import Existing Data To Help Create Shipping Labels Instead Of Creating Them One By One. Customer Will Be Able To Create And Save A Template To Be Used Again. Clicktoship Enables Customer To Import Files Of The Following Types:
    • Microsoft Excel Workbook
    • CSV Files
    • Advanced Text Files
    • Microsoft Access Database File
  4. Collection
    • A Uniformed Third Party Courier Will Collect Ready Shipments From The Address Mentioned, At A Mutually Agreed To Time.
    • On Arrival, Courier Will Reconcile All Shipments Against The Manifest, And Leave One Signed Copy Verifying Total Shipments Collected.
    • When Customer Requires The Third Party Courier To Do More Than One Collection On A Given Business Day, Customer Will Inform Third Party Courier Of The Same To Be Arranged.
  5. At Origin
    • Shipments Received At Third Party Office Are Packed, Scanned, Reconciled. A Discrepancy Report Will Be Sent To Customer In Case Of Any Discrepancies.
    • Shipments Will Be Sorted According To Destinations.
    • Third Party Courier Will Use Its Line-Haul Network To Send The Shipments To Their Final Destinations.
  6. At Destination
    • Destination Country Will Clear The Shipments From Customs.
    • Shipments With Values Subject To Customs Duties Will Go Through The Normal Clearance Process, Where Consignee At Destination Will Be Required To Provide Certain Documents Depending On Country Requirements.
    • Cleared Shipments Will Be Taken To Third Party Courier Destination Facility For Delivery.
    • Cleared Shipments That Are Sent To Third Party Courier Facility Pass Through The Below:
      1. All Shipments Will Be Scanned.
      2. Non-COD Shipments With Clear Addresses Will Be Sorted As Per Third Party Sorting Areas.
      3. Remaining Shipments Will Go Through A Calling Process Where Each Consignee Will Be Contacted 3 Times In 3 Consecutive Working Days To Verify The Address And Agree On Delivery Time.
      4. Upon Capturing The Delivery Address Of Consignee, Shipment Will Be Sorted According To The Area To Go Out For Delivery As Per The Agreed Time With Consignee.
      5. Consignees Have The Option To Collect Their Shipments From Third Party Offices.
  7. COD Management
    • Upon Delivery, The Courier Will Collect The Amount Of Cash For The Delivered Shipment(S).
    • The Cash Amount Will Be Settled To Accounting Department Within A Maximum Of 7-10 Working Days.
    • Accounting Department At Destination Will Make Sure To Update The System With Payment Details.
    • Perfect Line Sport Products LLC Will Settle Payments For All The Delivered And Updated Shipments During The Agreed Time Frames.
    • A List Of Settled Waybills Will Be Linked To The Cheques Issued To Customer.
    • Perfect Line Sport Products LLC Will Generate Internal Audits To Make Sure All Delivered Shipments Are Updated On COD System.
  8. Transit Time
    • Shipments Will Arrive To Destinations As Per The Agreed Transit Time Excluding Any Delays Related To Customs Clearance Process. Transit Times Are Not Guaranteed.
    • Shipments Will Go Through A Calling Process Where Each Consignee Will Be Contacted 3 Times In 3 Consecutive Working Days To Verify The Address And Agree On Delivery Time.

  10. Shipments Holding Period
    • Third Party Courier Will Have Different Types Of Undelivered Shipments:
      1. Consignee Refuses The Shipment And Requires The Order To Be Cancelled.
      2. Third Party Courier Could Not Reach The Consignee On The Provided Telephone Numbers In The 3 Different Attempts.
      3. Shipments Held At Third Party Courier To Be Collected By Consignee And Consignee Does Not Show Up.
      4. Consignee Asks To Hold The Shipment As S/He Is Out Of Country.
      5. Cash Is Not Yet Ready With Consignee.
    • Upon Completion Of All The 3 Consecutive Contact Attempts, Perfect Line Sport Products LLC Or Thorough Its Third Party Will Notify Customer Of The Last Status Of These Shipments On A Daily Basis.
    • Within 24-48 Hours, Customer Must Notify Third Party Courier What Is To Be Done With Each Shipment.
    • Customer Can Choose From Below On What Is To Be Done With The Undelivered Shipments:
      1. To Be Returned To Origin.
      2. To Provide Third Party Courier With Better Delivery Details In Case Customer Was Able To Contact The Consignee; Or
      3. Store Undelivered Shipment With Third Party Courier And Have An Integration With Our Inventory System.
    • The Maximum Period An Express Shipment Can Be Held At Destination Is 21 Calendar Days After Which Third Party Or Perfect Line Sport Products LLC May Dispose At Its Discretion Of It Without Further Liability.
    • The Maximum Period A Domestic Shipment Can Be Held At Destination Is 10 Calendar Days After Which The Third Party Or Perfect Line Sport Products LLC May Dispose At Its Discretion Of It Without Further Liability.

    • Upon Clearance, Third Party Courier Will Deliver The Returned Shipments To Customer’s Facility (Or Agreed On Location) And Capture The Name And Signature On The Delivery Run Sheet For Each Returned Shipment.
